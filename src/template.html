About me

I started my career in the hospitality industry as a chef and restaurateur where I quickly honed my management style, learning how to support teams both remotely and shoulder-to-shoulder. This start cultivated a deep understanding of perfecting small details while never losing the macro perspective of a product.

Finding my passion in people and marketing, I finished my master’s in business while working as an affiliate professor. It’s here I developed my abilities to instruct, guide, and lead large groups. After finishing my MBA, I narrowed my focus to program and department manager roles, learning to work strategically and cross-functionally. The various management roles I’ve held has led me to finding my passion in technical product management, a field that allows me to utilize my uniquely varied career.
